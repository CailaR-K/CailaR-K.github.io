USask Art Galleries and Collections

Redesigning an art gallery website for a more intuitive layout.

To enhance the online presence of the University of Saskatchewan galleries, I redesigned the website to prioritize usability and accurately represent our professional galleries, student gallery, and impressive collection. Prior to beginning the design process, I conducted research on current challenges and sought inspiration from leading international gallery sites. Using Figma, I created detailed wireframes and interactive prototypes that could easily be shared with stakeholders. Facilitating collaboration with the University of Saskatchewan IT team was important in this project.
